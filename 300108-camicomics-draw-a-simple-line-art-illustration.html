Draw A Simple Line Art Illustration











About:

Hi, welcome to this great Gig!



In this Gig I will draw anything you need with a simple and lovely line art style. Just bring your idea or reference and I'll do the rest. It can be a person, animal, item, building, landscape or object.



This drawins are hand made with a Wacom tablet in Adobe Photoshop, so they are unique pieces of art. You can use them on everything you want, such as t-shits, social media graphics, publicity, editorial proyects, packaging, postcards and more...



You will get a high quality JPG or PSD, 1000x1000 px, 300dpi. You can specify other size and resolution if needed. If you need it in vectors, I can do it in Adobe Illustrator, just let me know! I also can send it to you in PDF if you ask me so.



I am at your service so feel free to place an order now. Take a look at the Gig's extras to adjust the drawing to your needs. And of course, ask me whatever you want, we can talk about the details of your proyect and then I'll get to work.



Thank you very much!

Hope we can work together soon!

Cami.
