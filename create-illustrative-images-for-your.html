About:

Hi! 



My name is Jacqui and I'm a designer/artist living in rural Western Australia. I am an artist with experience in most mediums, however I always gravitate to watercolour illustrative artworks. 



I range from children's illustration to commercial design illustrations. I have worked on private commissions, architectural projects, posters, magazine  and commercial branding projects. 



Drop me a line and we can have a chat about what  I can do for you. Creating artwork is my biggest passion, I am not afraid of a challenge and I pride myself in customer satisfaction.
